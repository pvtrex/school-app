about Us

Welcome to Lakshya Institution

Lakshya Institution, located in front of SBI Bank, Mundia Navi Bux, Baheri, is a well-established educational center dedicated to shaping future professionals. Since its inception in 2014, the institution has earned a reputation for offering a wide range of career-oriented degree, diploma, and professional courses under a supportive and modern academic environment.

The institution provides programs such as LL.B, B.A.-LL.B, B.B.A.-LL.B, as well as degrees in management, commerce, science, computer applications, and pharmacy. It also offers diplomas and Ph.D. programs, catering to both undergraduate and postgraduate students.

With a focus on quality education, career guidance, and exam support (like IAS, PCS), Lakshya Institution continues to help students achieve their academic and professional goals. Admissions for 2025–26 are now open.

Chairman

Gaurav Kumar, the esteemed Chairman of Lakshya Institution, is a visionary and inspirational leader dedicated to empowering the youth through high-quality education. With a deep commitment to academic excellence and student development, he has played a pivotal role in shaping the institution’s growth and direction.

Since its inception in 2014, under his expert leadership, Lakshya Institution has experienced remarkable progress, consistently expanding its academic offerings and enhancing the quality of education provided. The institution now boasts a comprehensive range of professional and academic courses that cater to the diverse aspirations of students.

Mr. Kumar’s unwavering focus on discipline, excellence, and career-oriented education has guided numerous students toward achieving their academic goals and building prosperous futures. His emphasis on holistic development, character building, and lifelong learning continues to inspire both students and faculty alike.

Lakshya Institution proudly stands by its vision: “Where Education Meets Excellence, and Every Student is Guided Toward a Future of Success, Leadership, and Lifelong Learning.”
